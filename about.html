About Tendai

I am an aspiring game developer and student currently studying at SAE Institute Johannesburg and a very avid gamer. Games have always been apart of my life since childhood til now and what inspired me to make games was a quote that said "Be the change you want to see in the world", which inspired me to create the games that I and other people want to play as well. Some of my favourite games are the God of War series, Silent Hill, Uncharted and a new and current favourite is dispatch. The more boring parts are that I specialize in making 2D and 3D games, such as multiplayer platformers and 3D Co-op games that bring out the competitive nature in players. Additionally, I specialize in 2D environment and level design and sound design as I had a musical backgorund before branching into the dame development world.
